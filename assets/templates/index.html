Multi-website Advertisement Recommendation system

Abstract

This research shows a new way to make online ads work better with a system called MWARS. Many different online platforms are making it hard for advertisers to know where to put their ads. MWARS uses smart computer programs to look at what users do and like on many websites. The system takes information from many places to give users ads that fit them well.

The MWARS uses two kinds of ways to find out what users like. It also uses details from each website to make better suggestions. The system changes over time to keep up with how users act. They tested the system with real data to see if it gives good, different ads that make users happy.
